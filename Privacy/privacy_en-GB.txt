Sticky Frog Privacy Policy
Last updated: 17 March 2026

Thank you for playing Sticky Frog. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed when you use the game.

1. Controller
Sticky Frog
Contact: wcgames.turf741@passmail.net

2. Data the app may process
Sticky Frog may process the following data, mainly via Google Play Games services:

- Google Play Games account identifiers (to sign in to the service).
- Game progress and save data (for example: records, coins, unlocks and customisations).
- Basic technical device/service data required for app functionality and security.

3. Purposes
We use this data to:

- Enable Google Play Games online features (leaderboard/ranking).
- Save and synchronise game progress in the cloud (cloud save).
- Maintain app stability, security and operation.

4. Third-party services
Sticky Frog integrates Google Play Games Services, operated by Google.

Google data processing is governed by Google's own policies:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Play Games Services Terms: https://developer.android.com/games/pgs/terms

5. Storage
- Some game data is stored locally on your device.
- If you use cloud features, progress may also be stored in Google Play Games.

6. Data sharing
We do not sell personal data.
Data required for leaderboard and cloud save is shared with Google Play Games to provide those features.

7. Children
Sticky Frog is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child's data has been processed improperly, contact us.

8. Rights and contact
If you have privacy questions, contact:
wcgames.turf741@passmail.net

9. Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal or feature changes. The current version will be published here.

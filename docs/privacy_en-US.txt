Sticky Frog Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 18, 2026

Thanks for playing Sticky Frog. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed when you use the game.

1. Controller
Sticky Frog
Contact: wcgames.turf741@passmail.net

2. Data the app may process
Sticky Frog may process the following data, directly and/or through Google Play Games Services and Google AdMob:

- Google Play Games account identifiers and profile name (gamertag).
- Game progress and save data (for example: records, coins, unlocks, and customizations).
- Device or advertising identifiers (Advertising ID / AD_ID).
- In-app usage and interaction data needed for ads and measurement.
- Approximate location inferred (for example by network/IP), not precise GPS location.
- Basic technical device/service data required for app functionality and security.

3. Purposes
We use this data to:

- Enable Google Play Games online features (sign-in and leaderboard).
- Save and sync game progress in the cloud (cloud save).
- Show ads (interstitial and rewarded) with Google AdMob.
- Measure ad performance and aggregated usage analytics.
- Fraud prevention, security, and compliance.
- Maintain app stability, security, and operation.

4. Third-party services
Sticky Frog integrates:

- Google Play Games Services
- Google AdMob

Google data processing is governed by Google's own policies:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Play Games Services Terms: https://developer.android.com/games/pgs/terms
- AdMob Policies: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

5. Storage and retention
- Some game data is stored locally on your device.
- If you use cloud features, progress and related metadata may be stored in Google Play Games.
- Ad-related data may be processed by Google AdMob according to its own policies and retention periods.
- We keep our own data only for as long as needed to provide the service, resolve issues, and comply with legal obligations.

6. Data sharing
We do not sell personal data.
Data required for Play Games features and ad monetization may be shared with Google as a service provider.

7. Children
Sticky Frog is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child's data has been processed improperly, contact us.

8. Rights, revocation, and deletion
You can request information or data deletion by contacting:
wcgames.turf741@passmail.net

If you use Google Play Games, you can also manage or delete related data from your Google account tools and policies.

9. Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal or feature changes. The current version will be published here.
